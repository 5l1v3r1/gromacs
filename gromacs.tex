\section*{Preface \& Disclaimer.}
This manual is not complete and has no pretention to be complete, due
to lack of time of the contributors. It is meant as a source of
information and references for the {\gromacs} user.  It contains the
background physics of MD simulations and is still being worked on
which in some cases means that the information is not correct.
